Why Choose Us?

Our system is aimed to give nations a different view of Malaysia's stock market future. We will be the one that mainly focuses on Malaysian companies that are listed in Bursa Malaysia. We help investors and professional analysts to lower the risk of their investments by providing deep learning forecasts and provide an ads-free informative dashboard for investors and professional analysts.

How We Help

Our system uses deep learning architecture to analyze historical stock data and identify patterns and trends that can help predict future prices. This means that our forecasts are based on data-driven insights, rather than guesswork or intuition. We provide forecasts for a range of different time frames, giving you the flexibility to plan your investments according to your own strategy.

Who Are We?

We're a team of final year computer science students who are passionate about utilizing machine learning to solve real-world problems. We recognized the need for an accurate stock forecasting system in Malaysia, and set out to develop a solution that leverages the latest technology and techniques to deliver reliable forecasts.

What We Provide

Our system provides forecasts for a range of different time frames, including yearly, monthly, weekly, and daily. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to navigate and access the information you need.
